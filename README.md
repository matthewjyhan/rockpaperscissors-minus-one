# rockpaperscissors-minus-one
Available to play at [matthewjyhan.github.io/rockpaperscissors-minus-one/](https://matthewjyhan.github.io/rockpaperscissors-minus-one/)

Working game of Rock Paper Scissors... with a twist!
Also known from Netflix's Squid Game Season 2, Rock Paper Scissors Minus One is played by playing two hands at once, then removing one hand. 

## Features
- CPU matches, play at any time
- Multiplayer matches
  - Choose to play a quick match with random players, or make/join a private 1v1 room
- Score keeping
- Clean, simple UI/UX

### Example round:
**"Rock Paper Scissors..."**\
Player 1's hands: Rock and Paper\
Player 2's hands: Scissors and Paper\
**"Minus one!"**\
Player 1 removes Rock -> they're playing Paper\
Player 2 removes Scissors -> they're playing Paper\
**The game ends in a tie**

## Server
Multiplayer requires a server that is located at [rockpaperscissors-minus-one-server](https://github.com/matthewjyhan/rockpaperscissors-minus-one-server)
